Accessibility

Introduction

Pulse VR is dedicated to making our virtual reality experiences accessible to all users, including those with visual, auditory, motor, or cognitive disabilities. We value your feedback and use it to continue improving the accessibility of our website and services.

Our motto, “Virtual Reality for All,” reflects our commitment to creating inclusive, enjoyable VR experiences for everyone. If you encounter any accessibility issues, please dont hesitate to contact us at accessibility@pulsevr.com.

Accessibility Features

Here are our top 10 accessibility features that we have implemented to ensure that our website and services are accessible to all users:

Website Accessibility

  1. Keyboard Navigation: Our website is fully navigable using a keyboard, allowing users with motor impairments to access all content and features.
  2. Simplified Language: We use clear and simple language to make our content accessible to users with cognitive impairments.
  3. Accessible Forms: All forms on our website are designed to be accessible, including proper labeling and error messages.
  4. Mobile Responsiveness: Our website is fully responsive and accessible on various devices, ensuring a consistent experience across platforms.
  5. Color Contrast: We ensure sufficient color contrast between text and background to improve readability for users with visual impairments.

VR Experience Accessibility

  1. Subtitles and Captions: All VR experiences include subtitles and captions for users with hearing impairments.
  2. Adjustable Controls: Users can customize control schemes to accommodate different physical abilities.
  3. Comfort Mode: We provide a comfort mode & seated mode option to reduce motion sickness and discomfort for users sensitive to VR motion.
  4. Left-Handed Controls: Our VR experiences support left-handed controls for users who prefer or require them.
  5. Accessibility Support: Our staff is trained to assist users with disabilities and provide support during VR sessions. We can adapt our experiences to meet individual needs.
